    What does a Hospitality Consultant do?

    Hospitality Consultants are experts in their fields with extensive knowledge and experience in niche-specific subjects, using their expertise to address issues and solve problems found in businesses.

    At Spectra, our seasoned consultants leverage their industry knowledge to deliver tailor-made solutions, helping your hospitality business navigate challenges and capitalize on opportunities.

    We engage with clients during three crucial phases: conceptualization, development, and operations. During the conceptualization phase, we help crystallize your vision and lay down a solid foundation for your hospitality venture. In the development phase, we assist in turning your vision into a reality by overseeing project management, ensuring compliance with industry standards, and facilitating the smooth transition into operations. Once operational, our focus shifts to optimizing performance, enhancing guest satisfaction, and ensuring sustainable growth. Through a collaborative approach, Spectra’s consultants provide continuous support and expertise, ensuring your hospitality venture thrives in a competitive market landscape.

Our enduring commitment extends beyond mere consultancy; we partner with you through each phase, ensuring a harmonious blend of our expertise with your vision, culminating in a thriving hospitality venture that stands a cut above the rest.

    What is the revenue management strategy of Hospitality Consultants?

    Hospitality Consultants employ data-driven methods and adhere to industry best practices to scrutinize market trends, fine-tune pricing, and devise efficacious distribution strategies, all aimed at maximizing hotel revenue.     What types of hotels do Hospitality Consultants manage?

    Hospitality Consultants cater to a broad spectrum of hotels including full-service, limited-service, resorts, boutique hotels, and independent properties. The management engagement of these properties can be categorized into branded and unbranded management.

    • Branded Management:
      • Franchised: Hotels operate under a specific brand name and adhere to the brand or franchisor guidelines.
      • Management Contract: The brand assumes the operational management of the hotel, ensuring alignment with the brands standards and practices.
    • Unbranded Management:
      – White Label Management: Hotels operate independently without a brand affiliation, allowing for greater operational flexibility and customization.

    Spectra’s Role

    • Operations Management:
      • For Franchised Properties: Spectra executes the operations in adherence to the brand or franchisor guidelines, ensuring compliance while optimizing operational efficiency and guest satisfaction.
      • For White Label Management: Spectra takes on the entire execution of operations. We also create the brand guidelines under which the property will operate, ensuring a unique and compelling brand identity that resonates with the target market.
    • Asset Management:
      • For Branded Properties under Management Contract: Spectra solely engages in asset management. We serve as a pivotal bridge between the property owners and the brand operators, ensuring that the operational practices align with the strategic objectives of the property owners while upholding the brand’s reputation and standards.
